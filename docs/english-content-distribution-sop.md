# English Content Distribution SOP: Twitter/X + LinkedIn

> Applicable to: English content in `blog/en` and `knowledge/en`
> Maintainer: Gemini 1号 (Content Strategy)
> Last Updated: 2026-05-28

---

## 1. Overview
This SOP outlines the workflow for distributing English technical content across **Twitter/X** and **LinkedIn**. The goal is to maximize engagement, build authority in the AI/Tech space, and drive traffic to [peterclaw.com](https://peterclaw.com).

---

## 2. Platform Best Practices (2026 Edition)

### 2.1 Twitter/X
*   **Hook:** 70–100 characters. Must be bold or counter-intuitive.
*   **Threads:** 4–8 posts for educational content.
*   **Formatting:** Use aggressive line breaks. Native media (images/videos) is prioritized by the algorithm.
*   **Hashtags:** **1–2** niche hashtags max.
*   **Timing:** Wednesday and Friday, 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM (Local Time).

### 2.2 LinkedIn
*   **Hook:** The first 3 lines must "stop the scroll" before the "see more" button.
*   **Formatting:** 
    *   **PDF Carousels:** Highest performing for tutorials.
    *   **White Space:** Single-sentence paragraphs.
*   **Hashtags:** **3–5** hashtags at the bottom.
*   **Authenticity:** Share "lessons learned" or "build in public" stories to signal human authenticity.
*   **Timing:** Tuesday – Thursday, 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM (Local Time).

---

## 3. Content Templates

### 3.1 AI Tool Reviews
Focus on "The Verdict" and "The Catch." Avoid being a pure hype man.

### 3.2 AI Diaries (Build In Public)
Focus on "Expectation vs. Reality" and the "Human-in-the-loop" aspect.

### 3.3 GitHub Deep Dives
Focus on specific architecture patterns or "clever hacks" found in the code.

---

## 4. Workflow: From Markdown to Social

1.  **Extract Value:** Identify the "Hook" and 2–3 "Value Bites" from the Markdown file.
2.  **Draft with Templates:** Use the base templates in `docs/distribution-samples/english-social-media-samples.md`.
3.  **Tooling:** Use **Typefully** for X threads and LinkedIn scheduling. Use its AI to refine the hook.
4.  **Native Media:** Capture high-quality screenshots of code or tool UIs.
5.  **Engage:** Spend 15 minutes replying to comments immediately after posting.

---

## 5. Checklist

- [ ] Hook is within the first 280 chars (X) or 3 lines (LinkedIn)?
- [ ] Contains a link to the original article on [peterclaw.com](https://peterclaw.com)?
- [ ] Native image/video attached?
- [ ] Hashtag count follows platform rules (1-2 for X, 3-5 for LinkedIn)?
- [ ] CTA (Call to Action) is clear at the end?
