# PeterClaw Twitter/X Content Strategy & SOP (v1.0)

This document outlines the standard operating procedure for managing the PeterClaw English Twitter/X account, focusing on technical insights, AI tool reviews, and GitHub deep dives.

---

## 1. Content Strategy

The goal is to establish PeterClaw as a leading voice in AI-driven development and modern engineering workflows.

### Content Pillars
1.  **Technical Insights (GitHub Deep Dives)**: Analyzing emerging repositories, architecture patterns, and engineering excellence.
2.  **AI Tool Reviews**: Comparative analysis and hands-on experience with the latest AI coding assistants and productivity tools.
3.  **AI Squad Diaries**: Behind-the-scenes look at building and managing an autonomous AI squad (Multica).
4.  **AI Tool Tips & Tricks**: Quick, actionable advice for maximizing productivity with AI.

---

## 2. Publishing Rhythm

*   **Frequency**: 3-5 posts per week.
*   **Suggested Schedule**:
    *   **Monday**: Tool Review (Deep Dive/Thread)
    *   **Wednesday**: GitHub Deep Dive (Thread)
    *   **Friday**: AI Tool Tip or Diary Update (Single Tweet)
    *   **Saturday/Sunday**: Weekend Recap or Discussion Hook.

---

## 3. Formatting Rules

### Thread vs. Single Tweet
*   **Threads (4-8 tweets)**: Use for comprehensive reviews, architectural deep dives, or multi-step guides.
    *   *First tweet*: Hook + what the reader will learn + (optional) image/link.
    *   *Body*: One key insight per tweet. Use emojis for readability.
    *   *Last tweet*: Summary + Call to Action (CTA) + relevant hashtags.
*   **Single Tweets**: Use for quick updates, news flashes, or standalone tips. Maximize the 280-character limit but keep it punchy.

### Visual Rules
*   Every thread should have at least one visual in the first tweet (screenshot, diagram, or AI-generated image).
*   Use screenshots for tool reviews to show UI/UX differences.

### Hashtag Strategy
*   Use 2-3 targeted hashtags per post.
*   Primary: `#AI #WebDev #GitHub #SoftwareEngineering`
*   Secondary: `#Productivity #AIAgents #LLM #TypeScript`

### Tone and Voice
*   **Expert but Humble**: Share knowledge without being condescending.
*   **Action-Oriented**: Focus on "how this helps you."
*   **Transparent**: Be honest about tool limitations.

---

## 4. Maintenance and Iteration

*   Review analytics every two weeks to adjust the hashtag strategy and publishing times.
*   Update this SOP quarterly based on platform changes and audience feedback.
