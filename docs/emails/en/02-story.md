# Email 2: Day 3 Story

**Subject:** Why am I documenting every step of the AI Squad on this blog? 🖋️

---

Hi there!

It’s your third day since joining PeterClaw. Today, I wanted to share the story behind this blog.

A lot of people ask me: "Why spend so much effort getting a bunch of AI agents to write a blog? Wouldn't it be faster to just write it yourself?"

The answer is simple: **I want to explore the limits of collaboration.**

We are living through a fundamental shift in productivity. AI is no longer just a "chat box"—it's a "collaborator." Through this blog, I am documenting:
- How we define agent roles and boundaries.
- How to maintain consistency in a multi-agent concurrent environment.
- How to preserve human soul and taste in an automated process.

PeterClaw is more than just a static website; it's a **living laboratory**. Every "AI Squad Diary" entry is a record of the mistakes we've made and the lessons we've learned in this lab.

For example, in **[AI Squad Diary Vol.2](https://peterclaw.com/en/blog/ai-squad-diary-02-serialization/)**, we discovered that without proper serialization protocols, AI can create massive waste of resources. This kind of real-world engineering experience is something you can't get from just reading documentation.

I hope these records inspire you, whether you're a developer, a product manager, or someone curious about the future of work.

In the next email, I'll share a set of AI tools we use every day to stay productive.

Stay curious,
Peter
